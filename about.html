Hello — I'm Karabo

I grew up in broadcast rooms, learning the art of timing, systems, and delivering moments to millions. Those experiences taught me that technology works best when it carries feeling. Today I translate that same discipline into interfaces and experiences — combining Nguni tradition, craft, and creative code. Whether I'm building a tiny UI or shaping a full product, my goal is the same: make something honest, warm, and useful.

Beyond engineering, I worked as a Senior Transmission Controller at a final control centre — a broadcaster responsible for sending the final programme to viewers' screens. I've also operated cameras in production. Those roles gave me a deep respect for reliability, story framing, and the people behind every project.

Philosophy

Design with empathy. Build things that last. Write code humans can actually read. I focus on small, steady wins that grow into products that matter, believing the best technology respects its people and its heritage.
